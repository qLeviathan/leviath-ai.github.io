Our proprietary attention mechanism considers the phase angles, amplitudes, and distances between tokens to determine their interaction strength. This approach is inspired by how waves interfere in quantum physics, creating a natural way to model relationships between tokens.

Energy Conservation in Evolution

To ensure physical consistency, the QFNN enforces energy conservation during state evolution. Our proprietary normalization technique ensures that the total energy of the system remains constant throughout the computation process, leading to more stable and physically meaningful results.

Hebbian Learning

The QFNN implements a novel form of Hebbian learning for weight updates following the principle that "neurons that fire together, wire together." This approach is inspired by biological learning mechanisms observed in neural systems.

Our proprietary Hebbian learning approach relates neural activations and error signals to determine weight changes in the network. This biologically-inspired method creates a direct relationship between correlated activity and connection strength.

This approach bypasses the need for traditional backpropagation through the network layers, resulting in significant computational and memory efficiency. Our proprietary implementation ensures stable convergence while maintaining learning effectiveness across various tasks.

The QFNN learning algorithm achieves O(n) memory complexity compared to O(n²) for traditional backpropagation methods, making it substantially more efficient for large-scale models.
